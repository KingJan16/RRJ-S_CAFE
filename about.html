WE OFFER CLASSES RELATED TO COFFEE

A home barista refers to an individual who has a passion for brewing and preparing coffee at home with a level of skill and knowledge typically associated with professional baristas. A home barista takes the time to learn about different brewing methods, coffee beans, and extraction techniques to create high-quality coffee beverages in the comfort of their own home. They often invest in specialized equipment such as espresso machines, coffee grinders, and various brewing tools to replicate the café experience. Home baristas may experiment with different brewing recipes, latte art, and coffee beans from around the world to refine their skills and create personalized coffee creations.


A home barista class is a structured educational program or workshop designed to teach individuals the skills and techniques necessary to become proficient in brewing and preparing coffee at home. These classes are typically conducted by experienced baristas or coffee professionals and aim to provide participants with the knowledge and practical training required to achieve cafe-quality coffee in their own kitchens. this includes: Coffee fundamentals, Brewing method, Espresso basics. Milk frothing and latte art
