HOME BARISTA

A home barista is someone who has a passion for making high-quality coffee beverages at home. They invest in coffee equipment, such as espresso machines and grinders, to replicate the coffeehouse experience. Home baristas have knowledge and skills in coffee bean selection, grinding, extraction, milk steaming, and latte art. They experiment with different beans, roasts, and brewing methods to achieve desired flavors. Home baristas can expand their skills through online resources and communities. They enjoy sharing their creations with others and can personalize their coffee experience while saving money.

HOME BARISTA

In this course we will focus on understanding the fundamentals of what it takes to make delicious espresso, learning how to work to a recipe and adjusting your espresso grinder to achieve great results. You will get hands-on experience using both professional and home espresso machines, working on our Victoria Arduino Black Eagle and Sage Barista Pro machines.

We will also cover milk steaming technique, to achieve glossy, microfoam milk and look at lattes, flat whites and cappuccinos. There will even be some latte art practise, just for fun! We will also have time to chat about coffee, espresso machines and any other questions you may have.

LATTE ART CLASS

Latte art is the technique of creating decorative designs on the surface of a latte or other espresso-based drinks. It involves pouring steamed milk into espresso and manipulating it to form patterns or shapes. By controlling the milk flow and using tools, baristas create designs like hearts, rosettas, or tulips. It requires skill in milk steaming, pouring, and understanding the interaction between milk and espresso. Latte art adds visual appeal to the coffee and requires practice to master.
